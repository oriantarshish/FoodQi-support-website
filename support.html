Getting Started

FoodQI helps you track your nutrition effortlessly with AI-powered food analysis.

How to Use FoodQI

  • Scan Your Food: Take a photo of your meal using the camera
  • Get Instant Analysis: Our AI analyzes calories, protein, carbs, and fats
  • Track Your Progress: View your daily nutrition summary and history
  • Manage Settings: Customize your experience and preferences

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the nutrition analysis?

FoodQI uses advanced AI technology to provide accurate nutrition estimates. Results may vary based on portion sizes and food preparation methods.

Can I edit nutrition information?

Currently, the app provides AI-generated estimates. We're working on adding manual editing in future updates.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your nutrition data is stored securely and privately. We never share your personal information.

How do I delete my account?

You can log out from the Settings page. To permanently delete your data, please contact us at the email below.
